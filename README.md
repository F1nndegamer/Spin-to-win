WORM is a 2D puzzle game where you rotate the world's gravity instead of moving platforms.
Your goal is simple:
Find the exit without getting your tiny worm brain destroyed.
Gravity can be switched between all four cardinal directions, turning walls into floors and ceilings into your new path. Every level is built around figuring out when and where to rotate the world.
It's one of those games that's easy to understand but gets surprisingly evil later on.

Features
Play as a very intelligent (probably) worm.
Switch gravity in all four directions.
Puzzle levels that make you rethink the entire map.
Birds. We don't like birds.
Built in Unity.

Controls
WASD / Arrow Keys — Move
Gravity Keys — Rotate gravity (depends on your keybinds)

Made With
Unity 6000.5.0f1
